About Islamic Center

The Glorious Qur-an says, “Truth has arrived, and falsehood perished, For falsehood (by its nature) bound to perish”. (Sura Bani Israil-81)

What is Truth? The Bengali word “Sathya, is derived from the word Sat” which means everlasting, permanent, established, true, proper, correct, accurate and indestructible. In Arabic Al-Haq stands for the word Truth which is one of the greatest names of Allah. We worship The Truth. The opposite of the Truth is falsehood, untrue, inaccurate improper, incorrect, baseless, imaginary, destructible, deceptive and satanic.

The struggle between the Truth and the falsehood or the fight between Religion and irreligion is eternal and everlasting.
